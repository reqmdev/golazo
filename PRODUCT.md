# Golazo

Discord bot for amateur football leagues: fixtures, scores, standings, team management.

## Audience

League admins and players in Discord servers. They expect a **modern sports app** feel (FotMob, Apple Sports), not retro game menus or admin dashboards.

## Visual register

Product surface rendered as **clean sports cards** (PNG canvas attachments in embeds).

## Brand lane

- Dark neutral surfaces with subtle stadium photography
- Single green accent for live states and highlights
- Sentence-case typography, hairline borders, no neon glow
- Data-first, mobile-app clarity

## Canvas outputs

- Standings table card
- Fixture round card
- Match result hero card
- Help footer progress rail
